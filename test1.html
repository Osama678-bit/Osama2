FADE IN ON:

INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY

A rugged hand picks up a spoon and stirs his coffee with it. He takes the coffee and gently sips. This is MARK, 43, and with a tough exterior but as cool as John Wayne.

Across from him sits a fidgety man. AARON DECK. Aaron looks down at his coffee. A creamy brown. He looks back up at Mark.

AARON

So, how's Susan?

MARK

Good. She finally opened her haberdasher. So, she's enthralled.
